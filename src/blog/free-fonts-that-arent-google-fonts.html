Free Fonts that Aren’t Google Fonts

Google Fonts truly changed the web font game. It’s fantastic to have access to a variety of superb typography. And everyone else seems to think so too. Visit any website and you’re likely to find a Google Font. Now these are all beautiful typefaces, but since nearly every site uses Google Fonts I find them somewhat unoriginal. When I am looking for a font that’s unique and still free I reference the list.

  • Aileron
  • Archivo Narrow
  • Born
  • Clear Sans
  • EB Garamond
  • Edmond Sans
  • HK Grotesk
  • League Spartan
  • Lehigh
  • Medio
  • Mort Modern
  • Source Serif-Pro
  • Type Rubik
  • Young Serif
